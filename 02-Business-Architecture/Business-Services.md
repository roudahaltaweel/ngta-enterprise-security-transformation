# Business Services

## Purpose

This document defines the core business services provided by the National Government Technology Authority (NGTA). These services represent the technology capabilities delivered to UAE government entities and internal departments to support secure, reliable, and efficient digital government operations.

Understanding these services provides the foundation for future enterprise architecture, infrastructure design, cybersecurity controls, and service management.

---

## Business Services

| Business Service | Description | Primary Customers | Business Value |
|------------------|-------------|-------------------|----------------|
| Government Cloud Platform | Provides secure cloud infrastructure for hosting government applications and systems. | Government Entities | Enables scalable, secure, and centralized hosting. |
| Identity and Access Management (IAM) | Provides user authentication, authorization, and identity lifecycle management. | Employees, Government Users | Ensures secure access to systems and information. |
| Security Operations Center (SOC) | Monitors, detects, and responds to cybersecurity threats 24/7. | All Government Entities | Protects critical systems from cyber threats. |
| Government Network Services | Provides secure connectivity between government entities, data centers, and cloud environments. | Government Entities | Supports reliable and protected communications. |
| Secure Email and Collaboration | Provides secure email, messaging, and collaboration platforms. | Employees | Enables secure communication across the organization. |
| Backup and Disaster Recovery | Protects critical systems and enables recovery following incidents or disasters. | All Business Units | Ensures business continuity and service availability. |
| Digital Forensics and Incident Response | Investigates cybersecurity incidents and supports evidence collection and recovery. | Security Teams | Minimizes the impact of security incidents. |
| Governance, Risk and Compliance Services | Supports security governance, risk management, compliance, and auditing activities. | Executive Management | Ensures compliance with laws, regulations, and security standards. |

---

## Business Need

NGTA exists to deliver centralized technology services to UAE government entities. Defining these services establishes a clear understanding of what the organization provides, who depends on these services, and how technology supports government operations.

---

## Security Benefit

Each business service requires appropriate security controls to maintain confidentiality, integrity, and availability. Clearly identifying business services helps prioritize cybersecurity investments, risk assessments, incident response planning, and business continuity strategies.

---

## Assumptions

The following assumptions were made while defining the business services:

- NGTA provides shared technology services to multiple UAE government entities.
- Critical government services require high availability and strong cybersecurity protection.
- Cloud services are a strategic platform for government digital transformation.
- Security monitoring is performed through a centralized Security Operations Center (SOC).

---

## Summary

The business services defined in this document represent the primary technology capabilities delivered by NGTA. These services will guide future enterprise architecture, infrastructure design, asset management, and cybersecurity planning throughout the Enterprise Security Transformation Program.
