# Business Functions

## Purpose

This document defines the core business functions of the National Government Technology Authority (NGTA). These functions describe the organization's primary responsibilities and explain how it delivers secure and reliable technology services to UAE government entities.

Understanding these business functions provides the foundation for future enterprise architecture, asset management, risk assessment, and cybersecurity design.

---

## Business Functions

| Business Function | Purpose | Primary Departments |
|-------------------|---------|---------------------|
| Government Technology Services | Deliver shared digital technology services to UAE government entities. | Information Technology, Cloud Services |
| Cybersecurity Operations | Protect government systems, networks, and information assets from cyber threats. | Security Operations Center (SOC), Cybersecurity |
| Cloud Infrastructure Management | Manage secure cloud platforms that host government applications and services. | Cloud Services |
| Identity and Access Management | Control user identities and ensure secure access to organizational resources. | Cybersecurity, IT |
| Network Infrastructure Management | Design, operate, and maintain secure enterprise networks across all NGTA locations. | Network Infrastructure |
| Disaster Recovery and Business Continuity | Ensure critical services remain available during disruptions or disasters. | IT, Disaster Recovery Team |
| Security Governance, Risk and Compliance | Develop security policies, manage risks, and ensure compliance with regulations and standards. | GRC |
| Digital Forensics and Incident Response | Investigate cybersecurity incidents and support digital evidence collection and recovery. | Digital Forensics, Incident Response |
| Vendor and Technology Management | Manage relationships with technology vendors and oversee technology procurement. | Procurement, IT |
