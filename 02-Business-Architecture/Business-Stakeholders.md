# Business Stakeholders

## Purpose

This document identifies the key stakeholders of the National Government Technology Authority (NGTA). Understanding stakeholder responsibilities and expectations ensures that technology services and cybersecurity initiatives align with business objectives and government requirements.

---

## Business Stakeholders

| Stakeholder | Role | Relationship with NGTA | Priority |
|-------------|------|------------------------|----------|
| UAE Government Entities | Primary customers receiving shared technology services | Depend on NGTA for secure digital services | Critical |
| Executive Leadership | Defines organizational strategy and approves major initiatives | Provides governance and decision-making | Critical |
| Employees | Deliver daily business and technical operations | Use internal systems and services | High |
| Information Technology Department | Operates enterprise infrastructure and technology platforms | Maintains operational services | High |
| Cybersecurity Department | Protects systems, information, and digital assets | Implements security controls and incident response | Critical |
| External Technology Vendors | Supply hardware, software, and cloud technologies | Support technology delivery | Medium |
| Regulatory Authorities | Ensure compliance with national laws and cybersecurity regulations | Monitor governance and compliance | Critical |
| Citizens (Indirect Beneficiaries) | Benefit from secure and reliable government digital services | Receive public services supported by NGTA | High |

---

## Business Need

NGTA interacts with multiple internal and external stakeholders. Identifying these stakeholders helps ensure that technology decisions, security investments, and service delivery meet organizational objectives and stakeholder expectations.

---

## Security Benefit

Different stakeholders have different security responsibilities and access requirements. Understanding stakeholder relationships supports identity management, access control, governance, risk management, and communication during security incidents.

---

## Assumptions

- NGTA serves multiple UAE government entities.
- Executive leadership provides strategic oversight.
- Regulatory authorities establish cybersecurity and compliance requirements.
- External vendors support selected technology platforms under contractual agreements.

---

## Summary

The identified stakeholders represent the primary individuals and organizations that influence or depend on NGTA. Understanding stakeholder relationships improves governance, communication, and future security planning throughout the Enterprise Security Transformation Program.
