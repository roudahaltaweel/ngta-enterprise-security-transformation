# Mission

To provide secure, reliable, and innovative technology services that enable UAE government entities to deliver trusted digital public services while protecting national information assets through strong cybersecurity, governance, and continuous innovation.

---

# Vision

To become the UAE's leading federal authority for secure government technology, driving digital transformation through resilient infrastructure, trusted cybersecurity, and world-class technology services.
