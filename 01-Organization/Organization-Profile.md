# Organizational Structure

The National Government Technology Authority (NGTA) is led by the Chief Executive Officer (CEO), who oversees the organization's strategic direction and operations. The executive leadership team ensures that technology, cybersecurity, finance, legal, and human resources work together to support the organization's mission.

## Executive Leadership

- Chief Executive Officer (CEO)
- Chief Information Officer (CIO)
- Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)
- Chief Financial Officer (CFO)
- Chief Human Resources Officer (CHRO)
- General Counsel (Head of Legal Affairs)
  ## Departments

NGTA is organized into specialized departments that work together to deliver secure and reliable technology services to UAE government entities.

### Information Technology (IT)

Responsible for enterprise systems, IT operations, end-user support, and maintaining business applications.

### Network Infrastructure

Responsible for the design, implementation, and maintenance of enterprise networking, firewalls, VPNs, and secure connectivity between government entities.

### Cloud Services

Responsible for managing the government cloud platform, virtualization, cloud resources, storage, and backup services.

### Cybersecurity

Responsible for protecting NGTA's information assets through security monitoring, engineering, vulnerability management, and cyber defense.

### Security Operations Center (SOC)

Responsible for continuously monitoring security events, detecting threats, and coordinating incident response activities.

### Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC)

Responsible for security governance, policy development, risk management, audits, and compliance with cybersecurity standards.

### Digital Forensics & Incident Response

Responsible for investigating cybersecurity incidents, collecting digital evidence, supporting legal investigations, and improving incident response capabilities.

### Human Resources

Responsible for recruitment, employee development, and workforce management.

### Finance

Responsible for budgeting, financial planning, procurement approvals, and financial reporting.

### Procurement

Responsible for purchasing hardware, software, cloud services, and managing vendor relationships.

### Legal Affairs

Responsible for contracts, privacy regulations, legal compliance, and supporting organizational governance.
